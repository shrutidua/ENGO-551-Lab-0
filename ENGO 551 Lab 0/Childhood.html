My Childhood!

My name is Shruti Dua and I will share some facts about myself and my childhood:

I was born in Punjab, India on March 2, 2000.

I spent my earlier years in Punjab and moved to Calgary, Canada with my Family when I turned 7.

Most of my extended Family lives in either Punjab, Australia and Italy.

I always had a great interest in Motorcycles ever since I was little, so I saved up and bought my first motorcycle in 2019!

I have always loved helping my parents around the kitchen and now cooking and baking is one of my favourite hobbies.
